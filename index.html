Kenichi Ogita, Reo Yoneyama, Wen-Chin Huang, Tomoki Toda
Nagoya University, Nagoya, Japan

Demo

We used the Namine Ritsu's singing voice dataset [1], which consists of 110 Japanese songs by a single female singer.

Model Details


• SiFi-GAN [2]: Baseline model. Conditioned on MGC+BAP.
• VAE-SiFiGAN: VAE-SiFiGAN with F0-removal mechanism. Posterior Encoder was conditioned on MEL, Prior Encoder was conditioned on MGC+BAP.
• w/o Prior: VAE-SiFiGAN without F0-removal mechanism. Posterior Encoder was conditioned on MEL, and the prior distribution was set to standard normal distribution.
